About Us

As we know that Indonesia is the largest coconut-producing country in the world, our company utilizes this commodity to its full potential.
Our company cooperates with collectors and many farmers to provide various products from coconut such as semi husked coconut, white copra, and superior cocoa beans ready for export and local delivery. In addition, our company will continue to look for other abundant commodities to strive for maximum utilization of commodities.
